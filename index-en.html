Yildirim Salahaldin Hussein

A university professor at the College of Administration and Economics, University of Kirkuk.

He is the inventor and holder of the first Iraqi and international patent in the field of artificial intelligence algorithms, specifically for searching within unstructured archives.

He developed an intelligent program that utilizes AI techniques to analyze and search through documents and archives effectively. The patent was time-stamped using OriginStamp and officially recognized by the Iraqi Ministry of Planning. The project was presented at the College of Administration and Economics – University of Kirkuk.

He is considered one of the pioneers in AI in Iraq, with significant research and academic contributions in the development of AI applications. He is also a specialist and expert in statistics and programming.
